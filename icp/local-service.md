# ICP: Local Service Businesses

## One-line pitch
We build the website that earns leads directly — so you stop paying Angi and HomeAdvisor forever.

## Owner profile
- Owner-operated, 1–15 employees
- Revenue: $200k–$2M/year
- Tree service, HVAC, landscaping, cleaning, roofing, plumbing, concrete, gutters
- Spending $300–$1,500/month on lead platforms (Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack)
- Has a website — it ranks for nothing except the business name
- Technically averse: doesn't want to touch WordPress, doesn't want a project
- Motivated by: leads, phone calls, jobs booked

## Pain points
- Paying per lead and competing against 2–3 others on every one
- Website is outdated or was built by a nephew and never touched
- Has no service area pages — invisible to anyone outside their immediate zip code
- No time or budget for an agency; doesn't trust vague SEO promises
- Has good reviews and real word-of-mouth — the digital presence doesn't reflect it

## Pitch angle
Direct and no-fluff. Don't pitch "AI" or "technology" — pitch outcomes.
Frame: "You've built a real business. Your web presence doesn't show it.
We fix that and make it run without you."

Lead with the lead platform problem — it's immediate and visceral for this owner.
The landing page is the pitch. Get them there.
