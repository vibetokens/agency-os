## Rules (for AI generation)

```
Rules:
- Subject line first on its own line: Subject: <subject> — specific, observational, not marketing
- Short paragraphs. One thought per paragraph. White space between every paragraph.
- Mix of short punchy lines and slightly longer ones — rhythm matters more than length.
- No AI disclosure. No agency speak. No buzzwords.
- One CTA at the end — a single link with natural anchor text.
- Paragraphs as <p> tags with margin. No <br> tags. No bullet lists.
- Links: <a href="[LP]" style="color:#7c3aed;text-decoration:none;">anchor text</a>
- Tone: one smart person talking to another. Direct. Never salesy.
- State the offer, don't explain it.
- Sign off: — Jason
- Length: 150–250 words.
- Service examples must be electrician-specific: panel upgrades, rewiring, generators, outlet installation, EV charger installation, lighting, code compliance.
- Pain point: trades guys earn reputation through word of mouth but lose the "electrician near me" searcher to whoever has better SEO.
```
