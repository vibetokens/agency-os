- Mix of short punchy lines and slightly longer ones — rhythm matters more than length.
- No AI disclosure. No agency speak. No buzzwords.
- One CTA at the end — a single link with natural anchor text.
- Paragraphs as <p> tags with margin. No <br> tags. No bullet lists.

- Tone: one smart person talking to another. Direct. Never salesy.
